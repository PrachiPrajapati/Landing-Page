How do I book a Gig?

To begin, select the type of gig you’d like to book, either by typing in the Gigify Homepage Search Bar or from the All Services dropdown panel.

Three quick steps to make a booking:

1.Enter Gig Details

  • Gig Location: Enter the gig address, then drop a pin to show the precise address on the map.

  • Estimate of time needed: Take your best shot at estimating how time is required from start to finish. Don’t worry if you’re unsure about this estimate, the GigWorker will review this and get back to you their proposed estimate should they feel more time is required.

2.Choose Your GigWorker

  • You may view more details and individual reviews on any GigWorker by clicking the ‘View profile & reviews’ link. Once you’ve made your decision, simply Select that GigWorker to continue.

3.Review & Confirm Your Booking

  • You’ll always get to review all the details of the booking including cost estimate before confirming the booking with payment. For more information on how Gigify manages payments, visit our FAQ Section.

  • Your chosen GigWorker will be notified of your booking. You will also find a unique booking card in your My Gigs section; which is where you can chat with your GigWorker and manage all aspects of your booking.

How far in advance can I book a gig?

You can book a gig up to 15 days in advance.

How Do I Apply Promo Codes?

On the “Confirm & Hire” page, select “I have a Promo Code” to enter your code.

Notes:

Promo codes only apply to GigWorker hourly rate, they don’t cover additional expenses or tips.

There’s a limit of one promo code per gig.

Applying a promo code doesn’t affect the GigWorkers pay.
