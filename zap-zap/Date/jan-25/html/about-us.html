About us

About Us

ZapDeal is created and launched in January 2021 with the ultimate goal of changing the mobile marketplace industry. We want to offer to sellers and buyers a different aspect of what this growing industry can be to its best. We dedicated our efforts and hard work to give users the peace of mind well deserved when trading goods through this secured and easy-to-use App. Furthermore, ZapDeal is taking what use to be a neighborhood or local way of trading beyond the borders to facilitate users to market their products and make them accessible to various cultures and million more potential buyers. ZapDeal is a free App currently available in three different languages (English, French & Spanish) and can be downloaded from Apple Store and Google Play Store.

Easy-to-Use

ZapDeal is a friendly App that users will find very easy to use. Simply snap a photo, enter a short description, condition and location of the product, set a bargaining price and be on the way to display your goods to thousands of potential buyers.

The Peace of Mind

ZapDeal is a secure app that requires users to pre-register before posting. We integrated within the App a text messaging system to keep conversation between buyers and sellers confidential and safe from unwanted third party.

Being aware of fake posts that can potentially go through, we put together a robust team to constantly review posts that don’t adhere to ZapDeal regulations and Terms & Conditions. We want all users to know that “ZapDeal GOT THIS! and ZapDeal got their back as well. “

In Your Neighborhood & Beyond the Borders

ZapDeal is a local and international App that gives users the option to display their goods either locally or they can choose to select additional countries for their products to be displayed and to be seen. ZapDeal is currently working to partner with various international shipping companies to facilitate their transactions. However, seller and buyers can agree among them the most convenient way to complete their transactions outside ZapDeal support structure.
